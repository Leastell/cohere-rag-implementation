Shipping Information

Standard Shipping
We offer standard shipping on all orders with delivery within 5-7 business days. Standard shipping rates are calculated based on order weight and destination. Orders under $50 have a flat shipping fee of $5.99, while orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

Expedited Shipping Options
For faster delivery, we provide expedited shipping options including 2-day express ($12.99) and overnight delivery ($19.99). Expedited shipping is available for most items in our catalog, though some oversized or specialty items may have restrictions.

International Shipping
We ship internationally to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Delivery times for international orders typically range from 7-21 business days depending on customs processing and local delivery services.

Shipping Restrictions
Certain items cannot be shipped to specific locations due to regulatory restrictions. Hazardous materials, perishable goods, and some electronic items may have shipping limitations. We'll notify you during checkout if any items in your order have shipping restrictions.

Order Processing
Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days will be processed the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends will be processed on the next business day. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Tracking Your Order
All shipments include tracking information that allows you to monitor your package's progress. Simply click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email or enter your tracking number on our website's order status page.
